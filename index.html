Curecasa












Curecasa application is designed to be one of the most commonly used and preferred application to all users who needs any medical service provider to visit them at home, without the need to go to a clinic or a hospital away. The user will register and add his family members to be able to receive the service. The application covers a wide variety of service providers such as; doctors, nurses, asking for medicine from pharmacies or getting your radiology examination done at home. There is also the feature that reminds you of your medicine with its timing and usage. You can receive notifications about the latest promotions and offers that Curecasa offers. The application also offers the benefit of reaching the nearest entity close to your place. The user will have a financial record with the visits that came to him with the fees included. The application comes in English and Arabic languages to help all kinds of users. It has 2 different apks one is for Patients and the other is for service prodiver
